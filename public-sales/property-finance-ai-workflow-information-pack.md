## 01 · Client Situation

The client is an Australian property finance services business with a 20-30 person team and a small finance function. The team attended an AI workshop and wanted a practical follow-up pack covering:

- Individual follow-up learning for accounting / finance users
- Customised AI training for either the finance department or the whole company
- Options for building automation workflows internally versus having Metatree build them
- Microsoft 365 / Copilot implications for a finance-heavy business

The current workflow environment is straightforward but manual. Excel is central, automation is limited, and the team wants to improve efficiency without making a broad software purchase before the use cases are clear.

---

## 02 · Recommended Approach

Use a staged rollout:

1. **Training-led enablement**: train finance users and selected staff on safe, practical AI usage.
2. **Workflow audit**: map the manual finance workflows and identify the highest-value automation candidates.
3. **Pilot automation**: implement one or two workflows with human approval points.
4. **Scale only after proof**: expand Copilot, Power Automate, or AI-assisted workflows once the first pilot proves value.

This avoids the common mistake of buying company-wide AI licenses before the team knows where those licenses will save time.

## 04 · Microsoft 365 / Copilot Considerations

For Microsoft-based teams, Copilot and Power Automate are natural starting points. The most cost-effective route is usually a targeted pilot rather than a company-wide license rollout on day one.

Microsoft Australia public pricing checked on 2026-06-24:

| Item | Microsoft public price, ex-GST |
|------|--------------------------------|
| Microsoft 365 Business Standard | AU$18.70 user/month, paid yearly |
| Microsoft 365 Business Premium | AU$32.90 user/month, paid yearly |
| Microsoft 365 Business Standard (no Teams) | AU$13.90 user/month, paid yearly |
| Microsoft 365 Business Basic + Microsoft 365 Copilot Business | AU$40.40 user/month, paid yearly |
| Microsoft 365 Business Basic (No Teams) + Microsoft 365 Copilot Business | AU$38.00 user/month, paid yearly |

Recommended rollout scenarios:

| Scenario | Recommendation |
|----------|----------------|
| Finance pilot only: 3 users | Best starting point for workflow validation |
| Finance + managers: 8 users | Useful when managers approve finance outputs |
| Company-wide: 20 users | Only after role-specific use cases are proven |
| Company-wide: 30 users | Not recommended as the first purchase unless leadership wants broad AI literacy immediately |

Final Microsoft pricing should be confirmed in the client's own Microsoft tenant or with their reseller. Microsoft pricing, reseller discounts, Teams inclusion, and eligibility rules change.

## 06 · Boundaries

What Metatree can do:

- Map finance workflows and identify automation candidates
- Train finance and operations teams on safe AI usage
- Recommend whether Copilot should start with a small pilot group or wider rollout
- Build selected finance/admin automations with human approval points

What should not be assumed:

- Do not recommend company-wide Copilot licenses before the first pilot group proves usage
- Do not automate finance-sensitive decisions without human review
- Do not connect to banking, payroll, or accounting systems without written scope and access controls
- Do not treat public Microsoft prices as final reseller pricing
